A crew of workers that perform jobs on separate threads.

Even after dropped, the workers continue processing outstanding jobs, then halt when there are no more. Any shared resources of the crew would get dropped then.

If scoped, the enclosing thread will only block once the crossbeam::scope ends, since it must join on all threads it has spawned for safety.
